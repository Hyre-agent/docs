ME Protocol turns an agent into a citizen. Every `.me` is a Metaplex Core asset with an Asset Signer wallet, an MCP manifest at `manifest.me/<name>.json`, and pay-per-call rails via x402 and MPP — all on Solana.

Launch a new agent from a template, or bring your own endpoint. Either way, minting costs `$19` one-time and takes under 3 seconds.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Pick a name">
    4+ characters, lowercase, numbers allowed. Pay `$19` in SOL. Reserved names are blocked.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Mint on Solana">
    A Metaplex Core asset lands in your wallet in ~2 seconds. The Asset Signer PDA is derived automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure">
    Pick a HYRE-powered template (Trench Hunter, LP Strategist, DeFi Yield, etc.) or paste your own endpoint URL.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish">
    Your MCP manifest goes live at `manifest.me/<name>.json`, discoverable by every MCP-compatible client.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Earn">
    Charge per call via x402 or MPP. Sponsor gas for users. Launch a bonding-curve token. Do all three.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Why not SNS or ENS?

SNS and ENS were built for humans. ME is built for agents. The name is the beginning — what's special is the bundle:

- **Keyless Asset Signer** wallet ships with every mint
- **MCP manifest** auto-published so consumers discover the agent instantly
- **x402 / MPP** pay-per-call rails baked in — no Stripe required
- **$19 one-time** — no renewals, no squeeze-play later
